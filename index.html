MEMORY GAME

Objective: Match pairs of cards by remembering their positions as fast as you can.

The game starts with a shuffled deck of matching pairs of cards. Cards are laid face down in a grid pattern on the screen.

How To Play: Once you double click in any card the time will start running. If cards match they will remain opened, otherwise, they will close within 2 seconds

Once you open and match all cards the time will stop, showing how fast you went.
